About

Hi, I'm Topher. Welcome to DnD with Toph - a space where I share my love for Dungeons & Dragons, run weekly adventures and host a special DnD social club for children. All of this magic happens on Outschool.com, a dedicated platform for interactive, online classes for kids.

My personal journey with Dungeons & Dragons began later in my life. As a professional actor and a lifelong adventure games enthusiast, DnD was a natural fit. It gave me the opportunity to immerse myself in fantastical worlds, roleplay vibrant characters, and spend quality time with friends and family. Thanks to my background in improvisational acting, I found that DnD provides an ideal structure for anyone to unleash their creativity and build lasting memories.

If you're new to D&D, I invite you to start with my 'Creating a Character' class. Here, I'll guide you through the exciting process of building your first character. Once your character is ready, you'll be all set to join the thrilling adventures I host every week on Outschool!

These adventures, primarily designed for children aged 10 to 15, are the highlight of the week. Each 90-minute session is a quest in itself. Players battle powerful enemies, strive towards their ultimate goals, and eagerly anticipate the next meeting. Don't worry if you're a bit younger or older, we're flexible and believe that everyone should have the chance to embark on these amazing journeys.

For our young enthusiasts, I've also created the DnD with Toph club. This online forum, also on Outschool, provides a safe and exciting platform for children to extend their DnD experience beyond the weekly sessions. Here, they can engage in innovative projects like collectively building a city, complete with maps and characters, or compete in challenges to design the best monster!

Whether you're a first-time player or an experienced adventurer, join me. Together, we'll dive into extraordinary realms, forge lasting friendships, and embark on the adventure of a lifetime! Ready to start your journey? Head over to Outschool and let's roll the dice!
